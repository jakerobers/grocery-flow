# Salsa

A fresh and spicy dip perfect for chips or as a topping for your favorite dishes.

*tag1, tag2, tag3*

**4 Servings**

---

- *12* roma tomato
- *7* serrano
- *2* yellow onion
- *8 cloves* garlic
- *1 bunch* cilantro
- *1* lime
- *to taste* salt

---

Roast vegetables at 375F for 20 minutes (until the onions begin to brown).

Blend with cilantro.

Add lime juice.

Salt to taste.
